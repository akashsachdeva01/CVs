## Role Description
We are seeking an experienced **IP & Transmission Engineer** to support the design, implementation, and optimisation of telecoms network infrastructure. This role focuses on ensuring robust, high-performance transport networks across IP and transmission layers.

---

## Key Responsibilities
- Design, configure, and maintain IP and transmission networks  
- Support network integration, testing, and commissioning activities  
- Troubleshoot and resolve complex network issues across L2/L3 and transmission domains  
- Work with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless end-to-end connectivity  
- Perform performance monitoring, capacity planning, and optimisation  
- Produce and maintain technical documentation and network designs  

---

## Experience & Skills
- Strong experience with IP networking (routing, switching, MPLS)  
- Solid understanding of transmission technologies (e.g., microwave, fibre, Ethernet transport)  
- Experience with network integration and troubleshooting in telecom environments  
- Familiarity with network protocols (OSPF, BGP, VLANs, QoS, etc.)  
- Ability to interpret network designs and implement solutions accordingly  
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills  

---

## Desirable
- Experience working in mobile or fixed telecom networks  
- Exposure to network automation or scripting  
- Knowledge of network management and monitoring tools
